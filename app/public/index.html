FOUR VOLT

We are a web design firm located on Florida's Space Coast. We're collaborating with organizations and businesses to help them build powerful websites that engage users and produce results.

DESIGN.

We Do Our Homework

Before we write a single line of code, our first mission is to learn what you need. We'll work together with you to figure out who your target audience is and map out a plan for delivering your message to them - from concepts and prototypes all the way through launch day.

Custom Designs Just For You

Buying cookie-cutter off-the-shelf themes can give your site a cheap and impersonal feel, and they're often filled with spaghetti code that makes adding custom functionality a nightmare. We start with a clean slate and build a site just for you that will appeal to your audience and help you acheive your goals.

Websites Fit for All Devices

When somone goes to your website, you want it to work and look great regardless of what they're using to view it. We build responsive websites that automatically adapt to screens both large and small.

<DEVELOP/>

Levaraging Open Source

By making the most of popular open source projects like Wordpress and BackboneJS, we can deliver polished and robust sites in a quick and efficient manner, keeping you on schedule and your costs low. It's like having thousands of people working on your website for free.

Modern Technology & Standards

We love shiny new stuff. That's why we always strive to stay up to date on the latest in web development like HTML5 and CSS3. So, you don't have to worry about getting a website that looks like it's a decade old.

Keeping You Engaged

Nobody knows your audience better than you, so we'll keep you engaged in the discussion throughout the development process by allowing you to provide input on a regular basis. Your insight and direction keeps us on the right track and helps us deliver a higher quality final product.

DEPLOY.

Secure & Stable Hosting

We'll work with you to provide a hosting solution that is affordable, secure, and provides visitors to your site with a high quality experience. If you'd prefer to handle the hosting details yourself, we'll help transition the site that we've built entirely over to you.

SEO / Analytics

When we build sites, we bake in details that will make it easy for search engines to pick apart so that it will be easier for people to track it down. Additionally, we'll help you setup analytics so that you can keep track who is looking at your site and how they arrived there.

Social

An increasingly important component of web presence is social media marketing. We'll help keep you engaged in the internet discussion by making sure you have a solid and consistent presence on places like Twitter and Facebook, and we'll make sure that it's easy for your fans to announce how awesome you are to all of their online friends.

Montverde Academy

This was a full redesign of the website for Montverede Academy, including integration of news and current event info which had previously been hosted on a second separate site. We transitioned their multilingal content from a clunky old DotNetNuke site with a dated appearance to a more modern looking site backed by the WordPress CMS. This job also included a big expansion of the 360° Virtual Tour originally built for their MVASports site, expanding it beyond just athletic facilities to provide a broader look at their full campus.

MVA Sports

Our goal for this job was to provide an expanded presence for the increasingly successful and renowned Montverde Academy athletics program. Previously relegated to a single small subsection on the main Montverde Academy site, we built an entirely new domain and WordPress CMS powered site for them. This included 360° panoramic virtual tours of their top-notch athletic facilities.

The Soccer Institute at Montverde Academy

Another expansion of the web presence of Montverde Academy, this site was constructed to inform people about new soccer training programs at the school and put the soccer program's top-tier talent, training, and accomplishment on display.

Cruyff Court Montverde Academy

When Montverde Academy was ready to announce a huge deal with the Cruyff Foundation, they came to us with one day notice to create a site to present that info. We quickly sprung into action and created an independent page to highlight their big news. This page will likely be expanded into a more full-fledged site as this new venture for the school further develops.
